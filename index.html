How Does Coaching Work?

Life coaching is a wellness professional where a coach helps you make progress in your life in order to attain greater fulfillment. Life coach can help you clarify your goals, identify the obstacles holding you back and then come up with strategies for overcoming each obstacle.

Coaching is based strongly on cognitive psychology. Our thinking affects our feelings and thus our behaviour. Most of our thinking happens on subconscious level, meaning that we are not fully aware of most of our thoughts. By coaching we create awareness to your overall mentality and thus enable the possibility for you to realize your potential.

Reach Out

Initial conversation where I outline the coaching process in detail and we determine how I may help you. After this call you will understand how coaching can be helpful for you and what it takes to move forward on this path. Start by contacting me with the form found from the bottom part of this page.

Meet Matti

I suffered from bullying during my teenage years, going to school was something to be afraid of. Now in hindsight I feel it made me stronger in life. It made me realize we all have our own battles to fight, bullies included. Later on meeting new cultures taught me how to be open towards other people. Social anxiety shifted from being scary into being exciting and often rewarding.

I believe we all have huge potential hidden deep within, sparks that are just waiting to be revealed to us. Methods of coaching can give you access to that potential, a change to meet your true self. I understand what it takes to rise from low self esteem back into having healthy self-confidence. Coaching process and my own personal experiences give me the opportunity to help you to acchieve new levels in self-confidence.
